About Space Exploration

Our Mission

Space exploration is the use of astronomy and space technology to explore outer space. Physical exploration of space is conducted both by human spaceflights and by robotic spacecraft.

Since the beginning of space exploration in 1957, humanity has made incredible progress in understanding our universe. From the first satellite Sputnik to the International Space Station, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible.
